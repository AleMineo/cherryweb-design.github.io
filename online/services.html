Services

End-to-end talent booking, logistics, and fan experience services for standout comic con appearances

Celebrity Talent Booking & Appearance Production

With over 15 years representing and coordinating appearances for more than 200 celebrities, our team provides end-to-end talent booking and management for live events, conventions, corporate activations, and private engagements.

From initial outreach to onsite execution, we handle every detail to ensure a seamless experience for both clients and talent.

Services include:

  • Celebrity sourcing and booking across film, television, sports, music, and pop culture
  • Contract negotiation and deal structuring
  • Appearance logistics (travel, accommodations, schedules, security coordination)
  • Onsite talent management and liaison services
  • Autograph signings, photo ops, panels, and meet-and-greets
  • Fee consulting and budget optimization

Whether you’re securing a headline guest or building out a full roster, we bring established relationships and proven execution to every engagement.

Sponsorship & Licensing Strategy for Fan Events

With a decade of experience in sponsorship and licensing—particularly in motorsports and fan-driven brands—we help events unlock meaningful revenue through strategic partnerships and brand integrations.
We bridge the gap between event producers and sponsors, creating opportunities that are not only financially impactful, but also authentic to the fan experience.

Services include:

  • Sponsorship strategy development and revenue planning
  • Brand prospecting and partnership outreach
  • Pitch deck creation and sponsorship packaging
  • Licensing strategy and brand alignment
  • Contract negotiation and fulfillment oversight
  • Onsite activation planning and execution
  • Sponsor retention and long-term partnership development

Whether launching a new event or scaling an established one, our team focuses on building partnerships that drive both revenue and fan engagement.

Live Event Consulting & Production

From concept to completion, our team provides hands-on consulting and production support for live events of all sizes—specializing in fan conventions, pop culture events, and experiential activations.

Having helped develop and organize successful events for over a decade, we understand both the creative and operational sides of production—and how to align them for maximum impact.

Services include:

  • Event concept development and strategic planning
  • Budgeting and financial modeling
  • Venue selection and layout optimization
  • Guest programming and content scheduling
  • Vendor sourcing and contract management
  • Operations planning (staffing, crowd flow, ticketing, security)
  • Marketing strategy and audience development
  • Onsite production management and troubleshooting

Whether you’re launching a new event or refining an existing one, we help you avoid costly mistakes, streamline operations, and deliver an experience that keeps fans coming back.
