Talents

Connecting comic-cons with the talent fans want to meet

Talent Across Genres and Franchises

We work with a wide range of talent from across the pop culture universe.
From iconic actors and fan-favorite performers to ensemble casts and special guest groups, we help comic-cons book talent that truly resonates with their audience.
Our roster spans multiple genres and franchises — fantasy, sci-fi, medieval epics, cult classics, animation, gaming, and more — allowing each event to build a lineup that feels unique and on-brand.

Fan Experiences That Bring Talent to Life

Beyond appearances, we support a variety of fan-focused experiences. Whether it’s meet & greets, autograph sessions, professional photo-ops, stage panels, or special appearances, we help conventions secure the right talent for the right moments.
Every booking is tailored to enhance fan engagement and elevate the overall convention experience.
