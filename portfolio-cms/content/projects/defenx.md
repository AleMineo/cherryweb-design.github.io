<div class="description">
<h4>01 — Project Vision & Strategy</h4>

The primary goal was to create a corporate website capable of strengthening DEFENX’s brand positioning in the cybersecurity sector.<br>
The strategy focused on balancing trust, technical credibility, and accessibility for different target audiences — from enterprise clients to end users.

Key objectives included:

<ul>
<li>Communicate security and technological authority through visual design</li>
<li>Structure content to clearly explain complex cybersecurity solutions</li>
<li>Ensure scalability and easy content management through WordPress</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="description">
<h4>02 — UX & Information Architecture</h4>

I defined the information architecture and user flows to ensure intuitive navigation across products, solutions, and corporate content.<br>
Particular attention was given to simplifying technical messaging while maintaining depth and credibility.

Focus areas included:

<ul>
<li>Clear product and solution categorization</li>
<li>Conversion-oriented layout structure</li>
<li>Responsive navigation for multi-device access</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="description">
<h4>03 — Design & Development</h4>

I designed the full UI of the corporate website and handled its implementation in WordPress.<br>
The visual language combined structured layouts, strong typography, and a clean interface to reflect the company’s cybersecurity expertise.

Key outcomes included:

<ul>
<li>Responsive layout across mobile, tablet, and desktop</li>
<li>Consistent visual identity aligned with brand positioning</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="description">
<h4>04 — My Role</h4>

I was responsible for:

<ul>
<li>Corporate website UI design</li>
<li>Information architecture and layout definition</li>
<li>Full WordPress implementation</li>
<li>Responsive design optimization across devices</li>
</ul>

From concept to deployment, I ensured a cohesive, professional digital presence aligned with DEFENX’s positioning in the cybersecurity market.
</div>
