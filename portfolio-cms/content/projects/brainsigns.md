<div class="description">
<h4>01 — Project Vision & Strategy</h4>

The goal was to translate complex neuroscientific data into a clear, structured, and accessible tablet interface.<br>
Given the highly technical nature of EEG readings and brainwave analysis, the design strategy focused on precision, legibility, and ease of interpretation.

Key objectives included:

<ul>
<li>Design an interface optimized only for tablet devices</li>
<li>Ensure clarity in the visualization of brainwave recordings</li>
<li>Support professionals in analysis and monitoring activities</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="description">
<h4>02 — UX & Data Visualization</h4>

Particular attention was dedicated to information hierarchy and data visualization principles.<br>
The interface needed to balance scientific accuracy with usability, allowing users to quickly interpret charts, signals, and session data without cognitive overload.

Focus areas included:

<ul>
<li>Structured layout for EEG data visualization</li>
<li>Readable charts and signal representations</li>
<li>Clear navigation between sessions, recordings, and analytical views</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="description">
<h4>03 — Design Process</h4>

I developed high-fidelity UI mockups tailored specifically for tablet resolution and interaction patterns.<br>
Touch interactions, spacing, and typography were carefully calibrated to ensure comfort and precision during professional use.

Key outcomes included:

<ul>
<li>Tablet-optimized UI design</li>
<li>Clear and structured data dashboards</li>
<li>Consistent visual system aligned with the scientific context</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="description">
<h4>04 — My Role</h4>

I was responsible for:

<ul>
<li>UI design for the tablet management application</li>
<li>Information hierarchy definition for complex scientific data</li>
<li>Design of dashboards for brainwave monitoring and analysis</li>
<li>Optimization of usability for professional and research environments</li>
</ul>

My contribution helped transform advanced neurophysiological data into a clear and functional visual interface, supporting accurate analysis and operational efficiency.
</div>
