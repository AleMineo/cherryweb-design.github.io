<div class="description">
  <h4>01 - Project Vision & Strategy</h4>
  <p>Designed a tablet interface to make complex EEG and brainwave data accessible to medical professionals - balancing scientific precision with usability for analysis and monitoring workflows.</p>
</div>

<div class="description">
  <h4>02 - UX & Data Visualization</h4>
  <p>Focused on information hierarchy and cognitive load reduction, enabling users to quickly interpret signals, charts, and session data without friction. Structured navigation across recordings and analytical views was central to the experience.</p>
</div>

<div class="description">
  <h4>03 - Design Process & My Role</h4>
  <p>Led UI design end-to-end: defined information architecture for complex scientific data, built high-fidelity tablet mockups calibrated for touch interaction, and delivered structured dashboards within a consistent visual system suited to a research context.</p>
</div>
