<div class="description">
<h4>01 — Brand Concept & Positioning</h4>

Cameocraft operates in a dynamic environment that merges entertainment, fandom culture, and live events.<br>
The brand identity needed to reflect professionalism while remaining strongly connected to the world of comics, movies, and pop culture conventions.

Key objectives included:

<ul>
<li>Create a distinctive and memorable logo</li>
<li>Communicate authority in celebrity management</li>
<li>Align the visual tone with the energy of comic conventions</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="description">
<h4>02 — Logo Design</h4>

The logo was developed to balance entertainment appeal and professional credibility.<br>
Special attention was given to typography and visual impact, ensuring the mark would remain strong across digital platforms, event materials, and promotional assets.

The identity needed to feel:

<ul>
<li>Bold and dynamic</li>
<li>Connected to fandom and pop culture</li>
<li>Reliable from a business perspective</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="description">
<h4>03 — Website Design & Development</h4>

The website was designed to clearly present the agency’s services, represented talents, and international reach.<br>
The structure emphasizes clarity, strong visual sections, and intuitive navigation, supporting both event organizers and potential partners.

Key outcomes included:

<ul>
<li>Corporate website design aligned with the new visual identity</li>
<li>Responsive layout across devices</li>
<li>Clear service presentation and contact pathways</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="description">
<h4>04 — My Role</h4>

I was responsible for:

<ul>
<li>Logo concept and visual identity design</li>
<li>Website UI design</li>
<li>Front-end development and responsive optimization</li>
<li>Ensuring visual consistency between brand and digital presence</li>
</ul>

The result is a cohesive brand identity and digital platform that reflects Cameocraft’s role in connecting celebrities with global comic convention audiences.
</div>
