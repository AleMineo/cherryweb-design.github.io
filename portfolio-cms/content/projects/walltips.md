<div class="description">
  <h4>01 - Product Vision & Strategy</h4>
  <p>Walltips started as a frictionless mini app connecting hotel guests with local activities - no onboarding, no complexity. It evolved into a standalone platform aggregating experiences across the Italian territory, independent from individual hosts.</p>
</div>

<div class="description">
  <h4>02 - UX Research & Concept Development</h4>
  <p>Led UX research defining personas across traveler types - leisure guests, experience-driven explorers, short-stay visitors. Journey mapping surfaced three recurring friction points: fragmented communication, lack of trust in third-party services, and information overload - directly shaping the core user flows and feature priorities.</p>
</div>

<div class="description">
  <h4>03 - Design Process & Prototyping</h4>
  <p>Progressed from low- to mid-fidelity wireframes through user-tested interactive prototypes - iterating on decision-making paths and micro-interactions based on feedback. The product evolved across three formats:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Mobile-first web app</li>
    <li>Responsive desktop version</li>
    <li>Standalone platform with a centralized catalog of experiences across Italy</li>
  </ul>
</div>

<div class="description">
  <h4>04 - My Role</h4>
  <p>Owned the full UX/UI process, including:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>UX research and persona development</li>
    <li>User flow definition and information architecture</li>
    <li>Wireframing and interactive prototyping</li>
    <li>User testing and iterative refinement</li>
    <li>UI evolution across mobile, desktop, and platform versions</li>
  </ul>
</div>
