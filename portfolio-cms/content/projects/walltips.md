<div class="description">
<h4>01 — Product Vision & Strategy</h4>

The project originated from the idea of simplifying the guest experience while creating a meaningful connection between travelers and local communities.<br>
Rather than building a traditional hospitality app, I envisioned a frictionless mini app that could be easily accessed and used without onboarding complexity. 

The strategic objective was twofold:

<ul>
<li>Enhance the guest experience through clear, centralized information  </li>
<li>Create a digital showcase for local activities and small independent operators  </li>
</ul>

Over time, we added a host-dependent tool into a standalone platform aggregating experiences across the Italian territory.
</div>

<div class="description">
<h4>02 — UX Research & Concept Development</h4>

I led the UX research phase, defining user personas based on different types of travelers — leisure guests, experience-driven explorers, and short-stay visitors seeking quick recommendations.

Through user journey mapping and scenario analysis, I identified key friction points in how guests typically access local information: fragmented communication, lack of trust in third-party services, and information overload.

These insights guided the definition of the core user flows and the prioritization of essential features for the first prototype.
</div>

<div class="description">
<h4>03 — Design Process & Prototyping</h4>

I developed low- and mid-fidelity wireframes to validate the navigation structure and information hierarchy before moving into interactive prototyping.<br>
The prototype was tested with end users to evaluate clarity, usability, and perceived value. Based on feedback, I refined the interface, simplified decision-making paths, and optimized micro-interactions to ensure a seamless mobile-first experience.
<br>The product then evolved into:

<ul>
<li>A mobile-first web app</li>
<li>A responsive desktop version</li>
<li>A standalone platform independent from individual hosts, featuring a centralized catalog of experiences across Italy</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="description">
<h4>04 - My Role</h4>

I managed the entire UX/UI process, including:

<ul>
<li>UX research and persona development</li>
<li>User flow definition and information architecture</li>
<li>Wireframing and interactive prototyping</li>
<li>User testing and iterative refinement</li>
<li>UI evolution across mobile, desktop, and platform versions</li>
</ul>

From initial concept to scalable product vision, I shaped Walltips as both a user-centered hospitality tool and a territorial promotion platform.
</div>
