<div class="description">
  <h4>01 - Brand Concept & Meaning</h4>
  <p>The identity elevates wine to something almost sacred - the logo transforms a stylized red wine glass into a symbolic icon through three layered elements:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>A minimal red wine glass as the central symbol</li>
    <li>A golden-yellow circle forming the rim, evoking a halo</li>
    <li>Two stylized wings extending from the stem</li>
  </ul>
</div>

<div class="description">
  <h4>02 - Typography & Wordplay</h4>
  <p>The handwritten lettering "furio diVino" reflects personality and authenticity - essential for a sommelier's personal brand. The colored emphasis on "di" creates a deliberate double meaning:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>di-vino</strong> - of wine</li>
    <li><strong>divino</strong> - divine</li>
  </ul>
</div>

<div class="description">
  <h4>03 - Visual Balance & Style</h4>
  <p>The composition balances irony and elegance - the halo and wings add a playful narrative layer while keeping the mark clean, versatile, and strong as a silhouette across labels, social media, and print.</p>
</div>

<div class="description">
  <h4>04 - My Role</h4>
  <p>Developed the full brand identity: concept, logo design, symbolic composition, typography customization, and visual storytelling - distilled into a compact, expressive personal brand mark.</p>
</div>
