<div class="description">
  <h4>01 - Product Vision & Strategy</h4>
  <p>Econeth is a gamified crowdfunding platform for the gaming community - the design challenge was making contribution and campaign management flows intuitive while delivering a visually immersive, game-inspired experience across mobile and desktop.</p>
</div>

<div class="description">
  <h4>02 - UX Research & Concept Development</h4>
  <p>Mapped user journeys around multi-step crowdfunding creation and gamification mechanics - ensuring reward systems felt motivating without adding friction to core actions like contributing, commenting, and campaign management.</p>
</div>

<div class="description">
  <h4>03 - Design Process & Prototyping</h4>
  <p>Moved from wireframes to high-fidelity mockups and interactive prototypes, with key decisions including:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Bold, high-contrast visual language rooted in gaming aesthetics</li>
    <li>Step-by-step campaign creation flows with save, edit, and preview states</li>
    <li>Gamification features integrated to motivate participation without disrupting usability</li>
  </ul>
</div>

<div class="description">
  <h4>04 - My Role</h4>
  <p>Led UX/UI end-to-end, including:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Wireframing multi-step crowdfunding flows</li>
    <li>High-fidelity mockups and interactive prototyping</li>
    <li>Mobile-first and responsive desktop design</li>
    <li>Iterative testing and refinement</li>
  </ul>
</div>
