<div class="description">
<h4>01 — Product Vision & Strategy</h4>

The core vision for Econeth was to balance fun, engagement, and functionality. I aimed to build a platform that would appeal to gamers’ aesthetics while keeping navigation and crowdfunding flows intuitive.

The strategic objectives were:

<ul>
<li>Provide an enjoyable, visually immersive experience that resonates with the gaming community  </li>
<li>Encourage users to interact, contribute, and comment via gamified rewards </li>
</ul>

From the beginning, the focus was on creating a seamless process for users to launch, manage, and modify their crowdfunding pages, ensuring both mobile and desktop experiences were smooth and consistent.
</div>

<div class="description">
<h4>02 — UX Research & Concept Development</h4>

I began with wireframing to define the platform’s structure, focusing on clarity in multi-step crowdfunding creation flows and user engagement features.

User journeys were mapped to ensure the gamification mechanics were intuitive and motivating without becoming overwhelming. The goal was to make contributing and commenting straightforward while still reinforcing a sense of achievement and community through rewards.
</div>

<div class="description">
<h4>03 — Design Process & Prototyping</h4>

After finalizing wireframes, I moved into high-fidelity mockups and interactive prototypes for both mobile and desktop interfaces.

Key design decisions included:

<ul>
<li>A bold, gaming-inspired visual language with high-contrast colors and thematic elements</li>
<li>Step-by-step creation flows for crowdfunding pages, with save, edit, and preview options</li>
<li>Gamification features integrated seamlessly to motivate user participation</li>
</ul>

Iterative testing and refinement ensured the platform was both visually compelling and functionally robust, keeping the user at the center of every decision.
</div>

<div class="description">
<h4>04 - My Role</h4>

I led the UX/UI design process end-to-end, including:

<ul>
<li>Wireframing multi-step crowdfunding flows</li>
<li>High-fidelity mockups and interactive prototyping</li>
<li>Mobile-first and responsive desktop design</li>
<li>Iterative testing and refinement of user interactions</li>
</ul>

My contribution ensured that Econeth combined gaming aesthetics, engagement mechanics, and usability, delivering a platform that excites its audience while supporting effective crowdfunding.
</div>
