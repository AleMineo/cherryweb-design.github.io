<div class="description">
<h4>01 — Product Vision & Strategy</h4>

The objective was to translate complex blockchain and financial mechanisms into a clear and accessible user experience.<br>
Given the highly technical and regulatory-driven context, the visual strategy aimed to balance innovation with institutional credibility.

Key goals included:

<ul>
<li>Design a trading interface aligned with financial industry standards</li>
<li>Communicate security, transparency, and compliance</li>
<li>Create a scalable UI system for future platform evolution</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="description">
<h4>02 — UX & Interface Architecture</h4>

I worked on structuring dashboards, trading views, and account management flows to ensure clarity and operational efficiency.<br>
Special attention was given to hierarchy, data density, and readability — essential in financial and blockchain environments.

Focus areas included:

<ul>
<li>Structured trading dashboards</li>
<li>Clear visualization of financial data and transaction flows</li>
<li>Logical navigation between assets, orders, and account sections</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="description">
<h4>03 — Design System & Visual Language</h4>

The interface was designed using a modular and consistent component system.<br>
Typography, spacing, and color contrasts were carefully calibrated to ensure precision and usability in data-heavy contexts.

Key outcomes included:

<ul>
<li>Consistent UI components for trading environments</li>
<li>Clear financial data visualization</li>
<li>Responsive layouts for professional users</li>
<li>Dark and Light mode</li>
</ul>
</div>
