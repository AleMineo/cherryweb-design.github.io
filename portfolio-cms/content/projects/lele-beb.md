<div class="description">
<h4>01 — Brand Concept & Visual Direction</h4>

The logo was conceived to visually express the essence of a cozy Roman stay.<br>
The typographic mark “da Lele” becomes the central identity element, supported by a soft and friendly visual composition.

The concept combines:

<ul>
<li>The handwritten-style lettering “da Lele” as the main brand element</li>
<li>A coffee cup resting on a soft pillow as a symbolic graphic element</li>
<li>The tagline “bed & breakfast” to clearly define the hospitality positioning</li>
</ul>

The coffee cup represents warmth and morning rituals, while the pillow reinforces comfort and rest — together creating an immediate and emotional brand association.
</div>

<div class="description">
<h4>02 — Design Process</h4>

I explored different typographic balances and image compositions to achieve a harmonious and recognizable mark.<br>
Particular attention was given to proportions, spacing, and the relationship between text and symbol to ensure versatility across print and digital applications.

The final result is a logo that feels:

<ul>
<li>Welcoming and informal</li>
<li>Memorable yet simple</li>
<li>Adaptable to signage, print materials, and digital use</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="description">
<h4>03 — Additional Work</h4>

In addition to the logo design, I handled the customization of a WordPress theme to align the website’s visual style with the newly created brand identity, ensuring consistency across touchpoints.
</div>

<div class="description">
<h4>04 — My Role</h4>

I was responsible for:

<ul>

<li>Typography selection and icon composition</li>
<li>Brand application guidelines for digital and print usage</li>

</ul>

The project resulted in a distinctive and warm brand mark that effectively communicates the intimate and welcoming nature of the B&B.
