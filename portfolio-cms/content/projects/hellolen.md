<div class="description"> 
<h4>01 — Project Vision & Strategy</h4>

The objective of HelloLen\* Cosplay was to create a visually immersive portfolio that places imagery and character interpretation at the center of the experience.<br>
The website needed to reflect the artistic identity of the cosplayer while remaining clean, fast, and easy to navigate.

The strategic goals were:

<ul> <li>Showcase cosplay and modeling projects through a strong visual-first approach</li> <li>Create a distinctive digital identity aligned with the artist’s personality</li> <li>Ensure a seamless experience across mobile, tablet, and desktop devices</li> </ul> </div> 

<div class="description"> 
<h4>02 — Design & UX Approach</h4>

The interface was designed without the use of wireframes, allowing for a more instinctive and visually driven creative process.<br>
Layout composition focused on enhancing photography, maintaining balance between bold visuals and navigational clarity.

Key focus areas included:

<ul> <li>Image-centered layouts to maximize visual storytelling</li> <li>Clear visual hierarchy to guide users through galleries and content sections</li> <li>Minimal but effective UI elements to avoid distracting from the artwork</li> <li>Consistent aesthetic tone reflecting anime, manga, and cosplay culture</li> </ul> </div> 

<div class="description"> 
<h4>03 — Frontend Development & Interaction</h4>

The website was manually coded using HTML and SCSS, following a responsive-first approach.<br>
jQuery was integrated to handle interactive elements, transitions, and gallery behaviors, enhancing the browsing experience without compromising performance.

The implementation included:

<ul> <li>Fully responsive layouts optimized for all screen sizes</li> <li>Custom-coded gallery structures and dynamic image handling</li> <li>Smooth transitions and subtle animations powered by jQuery</li> <li>Performance-focused structure with clean, semantic markup</li> </ul> </div> 

<div class="description">
<h4>04 — My Role</h4>

I was responsible for the entire digital experience, including:

<ul> <li>UI design and creative direction</li> <li>Visual composition and layout decisions</li> <li>Manual frontend development (HTML & SCSS)</li> <li>Implementation of interactive features using jQuery</li> <li>Responsive behavior and cross-device optimization</li> </ul>

This project reflects my ability to merge visual sensitivity with technical precision, delivering a handcrafted portfolio experience that enhances the artist’s identity while ensuring usability and performance.
