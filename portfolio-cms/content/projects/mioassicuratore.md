<div class="description">
  <h4>Overview</h4>
  <p>Mio Assicuratore simplifies insurance management for both clients and agents - covering policy management, onboarding, and customer interactions across an agent intranet and a client-facing web app.</p>
</div>

<div class="description">
  <h4>01 - Project Vision & Strategy</h4>
  <p>The platform serves two distinct users - agents managing clients and policies, and customers purchasing and monitoring coverage - requiring separate but visually consistent experiences for each.</p>
</div>

<div class="description">
  <h4>02 - UX Research & Concept Development</h4>
  <p>Collaborated with the development team to define user flows and wireframes for both products - focusing on simplifying complex insurance information and reducing friction across onboarding, policy management, and purchases.</p>
</div>

<div class="description">
  <h4>03 - Design Process & Prototyping</h4>
  <p>Built high-fidelity mockups and interactive prototypes validated with internal stakeholders, delivering:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>New logo and mascot design</li>
    <li>Responsive web app for policy management and purchases</li>
    <li>Consistent visual identity across intranet, client platform, and marketing campaigns</li>
  </ul>
</div>

<div class="description">
  <h4>04 - My Role</h4>
  <p>Led design across both products, including:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Logo and mascot design</li>
    <li>Agent intranet panel design</li>
    <li>Onboarding flows in collaboration with the Angular development team</li>
    <li>High-fidelity mockups, prototypes, and client web app UI</li>
  </ul>
</div>
